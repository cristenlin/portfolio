Filmography

1
"Savouring Home"

Tools used: Premiere Pro.

This short documentary was created for a project in an undergraduate filmmaking class. It is the first group film production project I have worked on. I worked with three other students, all from majors that are not related to film production. I was an editor for the film, and I worked on dialogue editing, color, captions, audio, transitions, etc.

In this documentary, a family recipe reveals how identity evolves through food, becoming a vessel of time, experience, and fusion. Please enjoy.

2
"UBC Orchard Commons Dorm Tour"

Tools used: Premiere Pro.

I was the editor and main cinematographer for this YouTube video that introduces viewers to the different rooms and facilities of Orchard Commons, a UBC first year residence. This video was created with my friends in memory of our time living in residence together during our first year of undergraduate studies. As of Feb. 2023, it has received 12,000+ views and 260 likes on YouTube.

3
"Insomnia"

Tools used: Premiere Pro.

This short film was created for a 2-minute film project in an undergraduate filmmaking class. The limitations of the project were: has to be exactly 2 minutes long, and cannot have dialogue or music but must have sound. As an insomniac, the latter part of this project description immediately reminded me of sleep-inducing ASMR videos. From this, my short film "Insomnia" was born.
