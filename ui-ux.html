Equibuddy

Solo project
Duration: 2018 - 2019
Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop

This project was created as part of my university admission portfolio.

The Equibuddy app utilizes modern technology to facilitate people's relationships with horses. Paired with a smart halter (included in the portfolio), Equibuddy helps trainees learn how to approach horses and read equine emotions, monitors coaches' horses to keep them safe, and enables efficient communication between users so that everyone can cooperate to ensure the wellbeing of their equine-buddies.
