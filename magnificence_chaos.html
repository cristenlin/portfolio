Magnificence & Chaos

2019-2020
Painting

Growing up in the age of social media, I sometimes feel powerless as I surf the untamed waters of blinking pixels, flashing images, and shifting interfaces, pulling me every which way beneath the undertow. After I realized how detrimental social media is to my mental well-being, I decided to create paintings to explore my conflicted relationship with media, documenting my inner struggles as a teenager trapped in the social media maelstrom. Inspired by pop art portraitist Andy Warhol, I seek to do for social media what he'd done for popular culture, revealing both social media's magnificence and its chaos.

01
Tears

17.9'' x 15.0''
acrylic on gesso covered wood board
2020

My monochromatic, glum portrait contrasts “glitching” tears streaming down my face, revealing my desperation and inner fatigue due to pressures from the content I consume.

02
Mask

13.4'' x 23.5''
acrylic on gesso covered wood board
2020

Social media's captivating, vibrant mask hides most of life's dreary realities, as captured my portrait's two-toned face.

03
Compression

21.3'' x 15.6''
gouache, acrylic, and marker on card paper
2019

My portrait's colorful & sectioned WPAP style reveals fragmented emotions due to constant influxes of opinions and information from social media.

04
Blinded

15.8'' x 15.8''
acrylic on gesso covered wood board
2019

"Pixels" surround a monochromatic portrait of numbness, confusion, and blindness due to a digital world full of constant clashing stimuli.

05
Eyepiece

10.2'' x 31.5''
acrylic on gesso covered wood board
2019

My black-and-white portrait merges with a vibrant gradient of pixels while my identity merges with the digital world around me. A green "pixel" that covers an eye forces me to see the world through lens shaped by media.

06
Void

15.8'' x 15.8''
acrylic on gesso covered wood board
2019

My personas on social media merge with my personal identity, sucking the color and vibrancy out of the real me and leaving me fragmented and drifting in a flamboyant void.

07
Modern Zombie

15.8'' x 15.8''
acrylic and ink on gesso covered wood board
2020

Social media steals my attention and captivates my senses, manipulating me to wander about as if I have a malfunctioning mind. I meander aimlessly in a colorful yet chaotic digital world, staring at a display screen, going about daily life, or, more likely, both at the same time.
